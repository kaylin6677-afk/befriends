About BeFriends

👋

Hi, I'm Kaylin

I'm a 15 year old high school student who built BeFriends because I lived this problem firsthand. When I was learning English in Korea, I studied grammar for years but never had a real conversation with a native speaker. I always believed a real conversation would change everything — I just never had the chance.

BeFriends exists so that no student ever has to feel that way again — and so that no elderly person ever has to feel alone. I believe the most powerful thing in the world is a genuine human conversation.

Common questions

Is BeFriends free?

Yes — BeFriends is completely free for both students and companions. Always.

What happens after I sign up?

We'll review your profile and match you with the perfect partner based on your interests and availability. You'll hear from us within a few days.

How often will I talk to my match?

We recommend once a week for about 30 to 60 minutes. Consistency is what builds real friendships and real English confidence.

Is it safe?

Safety is our top priority. Every user is reviewed before being matched. We never share personal information without your permission.

What do we talk about?

We'll send you conversation topic suggestions before each call so neither person ever feels awkward or lost for words.
